5.0.0 HEDIS Test Data

To facilitate testing and verification, this guide includes test bundles for a number of sample patients, covering a range of measures.

  • Network – Practitioners, Locations, and Organizations common to test bundles
  • Patient – Patient profiles common to test bundles
  • Encounters – Bundles simulating patient encounters with providers over time

The Network and Patient bundles are identified at the top. The remaining bundles are all grouped by encounter and represent the histories of various test patients, with a Guid as their titles.
